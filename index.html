Privacy Policy — Sunrise - Alarm app

Last updated: 26 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Sunrise - Alarm app (package com.nurekedev.sunrise, the “App”) handles information. The App is a general-audience alarm clock and is not directed to children under 13.

Who we are

The App is developed by Nurbek (“we”, “us”). For any privacy question, contact us at: nurbek.nbs@icloud.com.

Our approach to your data

The App is local-first. We do not operate any server or user accounts, and we do not collect, sell, or share your personal information ourselves. Your alarms, settings, wake-up logs and streaks are stored only on your device. If you use Android’s built-in backup, that data may be backed up to your own Google account under Google’s terms.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App is free and shows ads using Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google and its partners may collect and process certain data, including your device’s advertising identifier (Advertising ID), IP address, and general app-usage and device information. This processing is performed by Google as a third party. You can learn more and manage choices here:

  • How Google uses data: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

You can reset or limit your Advertising ID at any time in your device settings (Settings › Google › Ads). If you remove ads via the optional one-time in-app purchase, ad requests stop.

Purchases

The optional “Remove ads” purchase is processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment details. See Google’s policy linked above.

Camera and device sensors

Some wake-up missions use your device hardware. Any use is on-device only:

  • Camera — used solely to scan a barcode/QR code for a wake-up mission. Images are processed on the device in real time and are not stored or transmitted.
  • Motion sensors / step counter (Physical activity) — used solely to detect shaking or steps for a wake-up mission. This data stays on the device and is not transmitted.

Permissions

The App requests permissions needed for a reliable alarm: scheduling exact alarms, running a foreground service while ringing, showing a full-screen alarm over the lock screen, posting notifications, ignoring battery optimization, and (only when you choose those missions) camera and physical-activity access. Network access is used for ads.

Data retention & deletion

Because data is stored locally, uninstalling the App removes it from your device. To clear data without uninstalling, use Android Settings › Apps › Sunrise › Storage › Clear data.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through advertising, contact us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above will reflect any changes.

Contact
